DavanaGere

Hitherto being a cotton hub and hence popularly known before as the Manchester of Karnataka, the commercial ventures of the city is now dominated by education and agro-processing industries.

ShantiSagara

Shanthi Sagara, also called Sulekere, is the second largest built lake in Asia. It is located at Sulekere of Kerebilachi, Channagiri taluka of Davanagere District in Karnataka state of India.


Harihareshwara Temple

Harihareshwara Temple, situated in Harihar, is a renowned pilgrimage site dedicated to Lord Harihareshwara, a unique amalgamation of Lord Vishnu (Hari) and Lord Shiva (Hara).


Santebennuru Honda

Santebennuru Honda is a picturesque water tank located in the Santebennur village of Channagiri taluk.


Kunduvada Kere

Kunduvada Kere is a beautiful lake situated on the outskirts of Davanagere city.


Glass House

The Glass House in Davanagere is a modern architectural marvel used for various cultural and social events.


Anjaneyaswamy Temple

Anjaneya Temple, located in the heart of Davanagere, is dedicated to Lord Hanuman.


Sri Durgambikadevi Temple

Sri Durgambika Temple, situated in Davanagere, is a revered shrine dedicated to Goddess Durga.
